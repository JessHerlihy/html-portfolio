Top Movies of All Time According to Jess

3 of my all time favourite movies

House on Haunted Hill

I really liked how lighting and music were used in this movie to build tension.

The Bat

I loved the strong female cast in this movie. I also was surprised by the twist at the end when the killer was revealed!

The Haunting

One of the best horror movies I've ever seen. Kept me on the edge of my seat all throughout.
